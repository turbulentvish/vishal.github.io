About

I am a computational scientist working at the intersection of applied mathematics, numerical methods, and scientific computing. My research centers on building reliable and efficient algorithms for partial differential equations arising in fluid mechanics, transport phenomena, and continuum models.

I am especially interested in high-order discretization, stability analysis, convection-diffusion problems, and designing methods that are both mathematically sound and computationally practical.
